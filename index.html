Feed my Starving Children

Introduction:

This project is part of our assignment in our Bootcamp course. We are supposed to choose a Nonprofit organization website and do the User research and UI redesign. Our group decided to choose the organization Feed My Starving Children because we love the impact they making and the good cause in the world so we want to help in a way the user can have a better experience till he is trying to get involved in helping this organization.

Task

Redesign the website so the user can have a better point of view of the mission, and impact of the organization.

My Role

Group members: Zorka Pretrova,Jimena Olivares Dora Montes, Candy Melara I served as a UX/UI designer. Working individually and supporting my group with research, user interviews, prototyping, and validation
